If you’re looking for a great movie then you must watch Fight Club. I watched this movie some time ago and it is a totally full of violent scenes and emotional experience. The movie “Fight... Club” is directed by David Fincher and starring Edward Norton, Brad Pitt, Helena Bonham, Fight Club told a completely heart-pounding scenes story. Fight Club is a crime, triller movie about characters who embark on a journey of fear and death, and Nietzsche’s revaluations of values. Despite the obstacles, big and small, the characters in the movie, as well as the viewers, still meet a great end. The performances from Edward Norton, Brad Pitt, Helena Bonham illustrate the narrative of this movie with such a viable effort. This movie is not only about fight club but also about a personality that built within himself, because of his work and stress and lack of sleep. A women called Marla appears in his life and then a man called Tyler. Consequently, in this film there is a plot twist. That scene will blow your mind.Fight Club intertwined visuals, audio, and plot in a very violent scenes way.It was as if David Fincher combined the characters, the setting, and the valued objects within the film. The cinematography, visual and colors were perfect.This story of fear and death, and Nietzsche’s revaluations of values. is a triumphant effort on David Fincher’s part in making viewers weep with surprise.Overall, I found Fight Club to be violent scenes, heart-pounding scenes, and gritty! It is a great movie with the theme of fear and death, and Nietzsche’s revaluations of values.
